About RSA SAS Training Course

This SAS training course will help you in understanding the domains of business analytics and business intelligence. Upon completion of the course you will have enough proficiency in reading spreadsheets, databases, use SAS functions for manipulating this data and debugging it. This training also includes data mining, modeling techniques, visualization of data, predictive analysis and extracting insights.

What you will learn in this training ?

Learn the SAS environment and various windows Understand the SAS Enterprise Guide Study the logical statements, operators and functions Create datasets and deploy Compilation and Execution Understand SAS graphs and summary reports Get to know Advanced SAS Base Course Completion Certificate from Intellipaat Be prepared for BASE SAS Professional Certificatio

What are the Prerequisites For learning SAS ?

Anybody can learn SAS and get trained. Having a basic knowledge of SQL can help.

Who should Take This SAS Training Course ?

Business Intelligence professionals Software developers and architects Project managers ETL, SQL, data warehouse and analytics professionals Mainframe and testing professionals Graduates wanting to enter Big Data analytics
